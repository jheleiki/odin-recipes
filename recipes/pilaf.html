Green Curry Seafood Pilaf

Description

A modern twist on the traditional Thai green curry, this one-pot wonder uses Zucchini Noodles to amp up the veg (whilst cutting back on carbs!)

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 4 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 cup (200g) brown rice
  • 400ml light coconut milk
  • 1 cup (250ml) chicken or fish stock
  • 500g seafood marinara mix
  • 250g pkt Coles Zucchini Noodles
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large non-stick heavy-based saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and spring onion and cook, stirring, for 1-2 mins or until the spring onion softens. Add the curry paste and cook, stirring, for 30 secs or until aromatic.
  2. Add the rice and cook, stirring, for 1 min. Add the coconut milk and stock and stir to combine. Season. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook for 20-25 mins or until the rice is just tender and the liquid is reduced.
  3. Add the seafood and zucchini. Cook, covered, for 5 mins or until the seafood is cooked through. Add the mint and coriander and stir to combine. Divide among serving bowls.
